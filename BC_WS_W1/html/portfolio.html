Portfolio

I've got a few series of images I worked on when I first purchased the iPad Pro 12.9". I started with a series of my favorite "Cheers" characters to learn how to use Procreate and test different styles. In May of '19 I began a Marvel series of the core Avengers characters as that was the time that Avengers: Endgame was released in theaters. I have also worked on various Spider-Man pieces throughout my digital art journey.

I've placed some of the images I've worked on below. There are many unfinished pieces. This gallery serves to display my work people that may take an interest in my style. Reach out and contact me if you're interested in having a custom piece done.
