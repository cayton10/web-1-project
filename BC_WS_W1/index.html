Benjamin Cayton: Amateur Artist

Welcome to my site. I've been attempting to get a gallery and information site off the ground for some time. I'm currently studying Computer Information Technology (CIT) at Marshall University, so my artwork has taken a back seat to make way for my studies. However, I wanted to create this site for anyone interested in my amateur artwork while also using this as a medium for potential commissions. I hope you like what you see!

Hopefully the Iron Man time lapse and the Marvel series caught your eye. The format for those in print are 11" x 22". Captain America is still a work in progress (wip). Sadly, I have many of those in my collection right now. If you like what you've seen here, check out the portfolio section, or contact me if you're interested in commissioning something.
